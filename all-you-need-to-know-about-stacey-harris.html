    Is Stacey Harris Married? Relationship

    On November 8, 2014, Harris married two-time All-MLB First Team selection Jacob deGrom, and life became a smooth sailing boat with vacations, baseball games, and evening strolls in Manhattan. Jacob met Stacey for the first time at a bull riding event, and something about her piqued His interest. So they kept seeing each other until he asked her to marry him on Christmas in 2013.

    The newlywed couple spent their honeymoon in Maui, Hawaii. They stayed at the Four Seasons resort, took a tour of Hana, and visited a volcano. Ziplining, paddleboarding, and snorkeling were also activities that the two enjoyed.

    Following their marriage, the couple settled in DeLand, Florida. They got a dog named Pixie, a cross between a Maltese and a Yorkie, to keep Harris company while deGrom was away at work.

    They now live in New York City’s Upper East Side. Stacey and Jacob enjoy exploring the city, trying new foods, and learning about the culture. In their spare time, they also enjoy strolling through Central Park and Times Square.

    Jaxon and Aniston are Harris and deGrom’s two children. Jaxon Anthony was born in DeLand, Florida on April 11, 2016. However, complications during his birth required him to spend some time in the hospital.

    How did Stacey Harris start her Professional Career?

    Stacey, a mother of two, worked as a sleep medicine professional while her husband continued to play in the minor leagues. In an interview with Steve Serby for the New York Post, her husband, Jacob deGrom, described her as a “Very Determined Girl.”

She resigned from her job after deGrom was promoted to the major leagues. As a result, she spends the majority of her time caring for her children or cheering on her husband at baseball games.
