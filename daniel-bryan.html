    Daniel Bryan Biography

    Bryan Lloyd Danielson, Daniel’s real name, was born on May 22, 1981, in Aberdeen, Washington, United States. He was taken to his father, logger Donald Orrin Danielson, and mother, therapist Darlene Jean Danielson. Jeri Ann Nagala, Kelly Marlene Sutera, Billie Sue Deck, and Jason Brown are his three sisters and one brother. He is 42 years old and was born under the sign of Gemini.

    Daniel Bryan Height, Weight

    Daniel Bryan Childhood/Education

    In terms of education, Daniel Bryan’s parents divorced when he was a child, and his mother raised him. He went to Aberdeen-Weatherwax High School and was very active in sports. He also decided to continue his wrestling training, which led him to enroll in Dean Malenko’s wrestling school.

    Daniel Bryan’s Wife, Marriage

    On April 11, 2014, Daniel Bryan married his professional wrestler girlfriend, Brie Bella. This couple has two children: a daughter named Birdie Joe, born on April 6, 2017, and a son born on August 1, 2020.

    Career

    Daniel Bryan won his first wrestling match in 1999 while still in high school. The following year, he signed a one-year contract with WWF. Daniel assisted them on their developmental tour and received training from William Regal, a talented wrestler and manager. After his contract expired, he traveled to Japan to compete in the junior heavyweight division of New Japan Pro-Wrestling. In 2004, he won the IWGP Junior Heavyweight Tag Team Championship there. Meanwhile, he worked for WWE from 2002 to 2003 despite not having an official contract. Similarly, he joined Ring of Honor and competed in his debut match for them.

    Daniel Bryan signed a contract with WWE in 2009 and competed in his first match against Chavo Guerrero, which he won. In 2010, he made his professional debut with his partner on NXT. Bryan has won and lost numerous games in his WWE career. Furthermore, when it comes to his victory, he has defeated many players and been awarded multiple titles. He has won the World Heavyweight Championship, the WWE Intercontinental Championship, the WWE Tag Team Championship, the WWE SmackDown Tag Team Championship, and the WWE United States Championship, the WWE Championship four times, and Slammy Awards 12 times. In addition, he was named Match of the Year in 2019 and has won the Twenty-sixth Triple Crown Champion and Sixth Grand Slam Champion titles once.

    Daniel Bryan’s Net Worth

    Daniel Bryan is a WWE pro wrestler who has amassed a sizable fortune throughout his professional career. His current net worth is estimated to be $10 million as of November 2023.
