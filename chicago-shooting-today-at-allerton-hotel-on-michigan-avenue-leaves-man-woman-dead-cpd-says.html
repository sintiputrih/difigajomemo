    Chicago shooting today at Allerton Hotel on Michigan Avenue leaves man, woman dead, CPD says

    Chicago police said a man and woman were each found unresponsive with a single gunshot wound to their head in the Allerton Hotel around 4:54 p.m. Chicago fire officials said the woman was found first, and the man was found about 30 minutes later.

    19 Jun 2024, 00:00
    CHICAGO (WLS) — Police are investigating after two people were shot to death inside a hotel on Chicago’s Magnificent Mile Monday afternoon.

    Chicago police said a man and woman were each found unresponsive with a single gunshot wound to their head in the Allerton Hotel around 4:54 p.m.

    Chicago fire officials said the woman was found first, and the man was found about 30 minutes later.

    The female victim was transported to Northwestern Memorial Hospital, where she was pronounced dead, police said. The male victim died at the scene.

    Police were seen investigating outside the hotel near Michigan Avenue and Huron Street.

    No one is in custody.
