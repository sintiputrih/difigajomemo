    BIOGRAPHY
    English actress Holly Earl made her television debut at the age of four, playing Robson Green’s daughter in Touching Evil (1997). She later appeared in the BBC Christmas special The Greatest Store in the World (1999). She made her big screen debut as May Bailey in Possession (2002). She appeared in the popular shows Casualty (2010), Doctor Who (2011) and Skins (2012). Earl also became known for playing the role of Zoe in the series Cuckoo (2012-2016). In 2012, she made her theatrical debut as Bertha in The Father at the Belgrade Theatre. She later received an Ian Charleson Award nomination for her role. We will soon be able to see her as the protagonist of Sea of ​​Blood (2022), a suspense film that follows a group of friends who enjoy a weekend with a pair of jet skis until they end up in a horrible head-on collision, which It leads them to struggle to find their way home.
