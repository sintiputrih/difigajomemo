    24 Apr 2024, 00:00

    The daughter of rapper Strongman, Simona Ama Osei has set the mood right for this Valentine’s Day after she showed her singing prowess in a new video shared on her official page managed by her parents.

    The little bundle of joy was sighted singing Akwaboah and Cina Soul’s Obiaa song finely tuned to the celebration of Val’s Day which falls tomorrow. The little girl who is barely three years old was seen with a makeshift microphone (face bush) singing and swaying her body to the rhythm of the song.

    The video was shared by her mother who is said to be the handler of her page along with a caption that read; “Somebody tell my daddy @strongmanburner to take me to uncle @akwaboahmusic shades of love concert on Sunday to perform this song with him❤️❤️.”

    In a related development, Afia Schwar is gushing over Simona Osei, the adorable daughter of rapper Strongman in a way that made her refer to the cute bundle of joy as her own child.

    The little girl who is barely 3-years old managed to tilt the pendulum of attention in her direction when she gave her own rendition of Cecilia Marfo’s ‘Whatshaway’ meme in a video shared on her official Instagram page.

    Afia reshared the video and stated; “Eiiiiiii so who is keeping my daughter in his/ her house ??
    Pls return her wai.. she is mine.”
