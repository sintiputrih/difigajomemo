    Career

    Kapoor got her first opportunity in the film industry.

    Through Teen Patti, a 2010 movie in which she played the character of a college girl. Despite getting a nomination for the Best Female Debut at the Filmfare Awards for Teen Patti, the movie failed at the box office. Her next project was Luv Ka The End, in which she played the part of a teenage girl who plans revenge on her boyfriend.

    Later, she signed one of the biggest and most important projects of her life “Aashiqui 2” alongside Aditya Roy Kapoor in 2013. She played as Arohi (a small-town singer who achieves fame and success after meeting a popular male singer). The movie was a big hit and it earned over INR 1.09 billion. The same year, she played a brief role in Gori Tere Pyar Mein alongside Emraan Khan and Kareena Kapoor.

    Shraddha was featured in Ek Villain opposite Siddhartha Malhotra in 2014.

    She also recorded the song “Galliyan” for the movie. This became Shraddha’s one of the most successful projects. It earned a whopping revenue of over 1 billion. Shraddha was then starred in Haider alongside Tabbu and Shahid Kapoor. She also got a chance to perform her first item song in Ungli. In the subsequent year, Shraddha Kapoor appeared in ABCD 2 (Any Body Can Dance), in which she played the part of a hip-hop dancer taking part in the international dance competition. Remo D’Souza and Prabhudeva taught Shraddha unique dance forms for this movie. Crossing INR 1.57 billion, ABCD 2 became one of the most popular movies featuring Shraddha.

    Her next project was Baaghi, where she worked with Tiger Shroff. This romantic action drama performed incredibly well at the box office with the revenue collection exceeding INR 1.25 Crores. Shraddha got training in the martial art for Baaghi. Later that year, the sequel to Rock On movie featuring Shraddha Kapoor was released. However, the movie flopped.

    Her first film in 2017 was Ok Jaanu in which, she got another opportunity to work with her Aashiqui 2 co-star Aditya Roy Kapoor. Shraddha had 3 movies released in 2017. In addition to Ok Jaanu, she starred in Haseena Parkar and Half Girlfriend. Unfortunately, none of her movies performed well at the box office that year.

    Kapoor’s next movie was opposite Rajkumar Rao. In this 2018 comedy horror movie, Shraddha played the part of an unnamed and mysterious woman. Fortunately, the movie became a commercially successful film at the box office with the revenues crossing INR 1.7 billion. Shraddha reunited with Shahid Kapoor for her next project “Batti Gul Meter Chaalu” that focused on the electricity issues in India.

    Kapoor had to play the role of Saina Nehwal in a biopic. However, she declined the project after a few days of shooting as she had other projects lined up. Shraddha appeared with the Bahubali star “Prabhas” in Saho. The film was a commercial success. Shraddha’s last hit was Chhichhore opposite Sushant Singh Rajput. The movie earned INR 2 billion and became one of the highest-grossing films of the year. She appeared in Baaghi 3 and Street Dancer 3D, however, both movies underperformed.

    Controversy

Shraddha caught another controversy in 2020 when her name appeared in the drug scandal after Sushant Singh Rajput’s death. The actress was questioned by Narcotics Control Bureau.
