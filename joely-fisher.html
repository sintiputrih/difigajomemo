    Bio:
    Joely Fisher (born October 29, 1967 in Burbank, California) is an American actress and singer. Her first film was the comedy Pretty Smart in ’86. She also starred in I’ll Do Anything with Nick Nolte in 1994. Joely was cast as Paige Clark on the sitcom Ellen, a role for which she received a Golden Globe nomination. Joely made her Broadway debut in 1994 as an understudy in the revival of Grease. She was also a replacement in the revival of Cabaret in 1998. She most recently starred as Joy Stark in the Fox TV show ‘Til Death.

    Best Known For:
    Joely is best known for her acting and singing. She is also known as the daughter of Eddie Fisher and Connie Stevens.

    Personal Life:
    Joely is the daughter of Old Hollywood actress Connie Stevens, and singer Eddie Fisher and the sister of actress Tricia Fisher. She is the half sister to Carrie Fisher and Todd Fisher. Joely married cinematographer Christopher Duddy in 1996. The couple has three daughters — Skylar Grace (2001), True Harlow (2006) and Olivia Luna (adopted in 2008).  The family lives in LA next to Joely’s sister Tricia.
