    Update, 9/22/15: Gwyneth Paltrow and Brad Falchuk are taking their relationship to the next level—Instagram, that is. The typically low-key couple stepped out together last night for the premiere of Falchuk's show, Scream Queens, in Los Angeles and even shared a sweet selfie together from inside the premiere. Falchuk posted a black and white selfie with Paltrow captioning it, "My date and I #screamqueens." Paltrow and Falchuk have been quietly dating for about a year now, but this marks their first Instagram post together—which of course, is a pivotal moment in any modern-day relationship. 

    -Lauren Fisher

    Original Post, August 20, 2014: Despite signs pointing to a reunion between Gwyneth Paltrow and Chris Martin (the duo spent much of the summer together in the Hamptons), it looks like the actress might finally be moving on. Us Weekly reports that Paltrow is dating Brad Falchuk, the co-creator of Glee. The couple were spotted sharing a "flirty" meal in LA last month, and later traveled to Utah for a quick weekend vacation on July 25. In the past four years, Paltrow guest-starred on five episodes of Glee, where "things started off professionally," with Falchuk, a source told UsWeekly. Like Paltrow, Falchuk has two children, a son and a daughter, from a previous marriage.
