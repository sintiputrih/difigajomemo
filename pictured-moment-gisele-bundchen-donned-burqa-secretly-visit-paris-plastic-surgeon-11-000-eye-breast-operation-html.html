    07 Jul 2024, 00:00

    These photos show former supermodel Gisele Bündchen donning a burqa in an unsuccessful bid to evade the paparazzi as she checked into a famous plastic surgery clinic in Paris earlier this month.

    In the images, Bündchen and her younger sister, Rafaela, are pictured stepping out of a black Mercedes outside Clinique du Parc Monceau on July 15 dressed in the traditional Muslim wear.

    Thanks to the head-to-toe black garment, the only features of the recently-retired supermodel that are visible are her feet. But her identity is given away by the presence of her longtime chauffeur.

    Just a day before the driver dropped off the two women at the clinic, Bündchen - who reportedly got plastic surgery at the facility - was seen elsewhere in Paris with her chauffeur of at least five years.

    The sisters also clearly did not do their research on the culture, as they are both seen wearing open-toed sandals in the photos, which some Muslim women consider inappropriate to wear in public.

    Additionally, France has banned the wearing of burqas in public spaces since 2010. 

    The images were snapped on July 15 - two days before the end of the holy month of Ramadan.

    According to sources who spoke to the New York Post, Bündchen and her sister underwent overnight plastic surgery procedures after posing as devout Muslims to secretly enter the clinic. 

    Following the claims, revealed Wednesday, Bündchen was accused of misappropriating the burqa.

    'She’s not doing it for Islam. This is very bad,' Imam Muhammad Abdullah Kamal Al-Azhari of the Astoria Islamic Center in Queens told Page Six.

    'She’s wearing a holy thing for a bad purpose.'

    Residents of the city are also angry, with one commenting on a French website; 'To be photographed in a burqa is ridiculous!!!! Especially since it is forbidden in France to wear it!!!!'

    They then added: 'She would do better to fess up to the plastic surgery because that’s not shameful. I never found her to be beautiful and I definitely don’t like her now.'
