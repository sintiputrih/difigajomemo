    Tyga's lashing out! Looks like Kylie Jenner's boyfriend is unhappy with his ex Blac Chyna, who he seems to think is telling lies about him, and the birthday present he bought his girlfriend. 

    The feud started on Saturday, Sept 5, when Jenner's boyfriend shared a serious-looking selfie of himself via Instagram with the caption, "When the hating don't work they start telling lies. #WhereYoFerarriTho?"

    The cryptic message was not initially obviously about Chyna, Tyga's ex-girlfriend and the mother of his 2-year-old son, King Cairo. But when Chyna's best pal Amber Rose piped up with a response, things started to become clearer. 

    Sharing a photograph of her bright pink Ferrari, Chyna's best buddy captioned the snap, "She don't need a Ferrari she can Drive mine," adding the hashtag BFFGoals. 

    Swiftly, Tyga responded with a picture of his super-shiny, gold-fronted Bentley and a very pointed message to Rose. "We drive our own whips peanut head #GoldPhantoms #LastKings," he wrote. 

    According to TMZ, the source of the beef is accusations Tyga is receiving that he didn't pay outright for Jenner's fancy birthday gift, but is instead leasing the $260,000 Ferrari 458 convertible he presented to the Keeping Up With the Kardashians star outside her 18th birthday party in Los Angeles back in August. 

    Neither Chyna nor Jenner have commented directly on the cryptic Instagrams, with both spending the past 24-hours sharing photographs of themselves in fairly revealing, busty outfits. But while the ladies in his life are taking the high road for now, something tells Us this feud isn't over!
