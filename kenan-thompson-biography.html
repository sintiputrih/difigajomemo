    Who is Kenan Thompson?

    Kenan Thompson is an American actor, comedian, producer, and judge. He is popular for his work on Saturday Night Live.

    He makes impressions of celebrities including Al Sharpton, Charles Barkley, Steve Harvey, and David Ortiz.

    Besides, he also does podcasts in @youalreadyknowpodcast.

    Who are the parents of Kenan Thompson? Bio, Age, Parents

    Kenan Thompson was born on May 10, 1978, in Atlanta, Georgia, USA, His ethnicity is All-American.

    He is the son of Fletcher (father) and Elizabeth Ann Thompson (mother). Kenan has two siblings, an older brother, and a younger sister.

    Talking about education, Kenan went to Tri-Cities High School in East Point, Georgia.

    Kenan Thompson: Professional Life, Career

    Kenan Thompson’s work was rewarded in his teens when he won a role at age 16, in the 1994 film D2: The Mighty Ducks. That same year, he began appearing on the popular Nickelodeon show All That.

    In 1996, he and his co-star Kel Mitchell began starring in a highly successful spin-off, Kenan and Kel, which lasted four seasons and helped earn Thompson a Kids’ Choice Award in 1998.

    After leaving Nickelodeon, Thompson appeared in a number of films, including Rocky and Bullwinkle (2000), Love Don’t Cost a Thing (2003), Barbershop 2 (2004), and Snakes on a Plane (2006).

    In 2004, he also starred in the film Fat Albert, working alongside his childhood idol, Bill Cosby. In 2003, Thompson won a role on Saturday Night Live and is still associated with that show.

    Moreover, in Sep 2015, he appeared on The Tonight Show Starring Jimmy Fallon.

    Thompson co-founded the production company and talent incubator Artist for Artists (AFA).

    He plays Ron, a friend of the Kids in the Hallin the third episode of the return of The Kids in the Hall. Also, he will be the host of the NHL Awards in June 2022.

    Kenan set a record on the Oscar Issac-hosted program on March 5, 2022, in which he appeared in more than 1500 sketches.

    Recent Movies and TV Shows

    Kenan has voiced for animated movies like The Grinch (2018), Wonder Park (2019), Playmobil: The Movie (2019), and Trolls World Tour (2020).

    Besides this, his TV projects are Double Dare (2018) as himself, The Masked Singer (2019) as a guest judge, Bring the Funny (2019) as the judge, and The Kenan Show (2020).

    Awards

    • In 2018, he won the Emmy Award for Outstanding Original Music and Lyrics for the “SNL” song “Come Back, Barack.”
    • In 2018 and 2020, he was nominated for Outstanding Supporting Actor in a Comedy Series.
    • Also, in 2017, he received an Emmy nomination for co-writing “Last Christmas” from the popular sketch Jingle Barack.
