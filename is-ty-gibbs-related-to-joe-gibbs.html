    Ty Gibbs has progressed quickly through the NASCAR ranks, but he has begun to establish a reputation for his driving and name, leaving him on a short leash.

    The debut of Ty Gibbs in NASCAR couldn’t have gone unnoticed.

    In February 2021, he made his Xfinity Series debut at Daytona International Speedway, defeating incumbent series champion Austin Cindric.

    While Gibbs’ promising debut put him on the map, he was swiftly met with criticism.

    Gibbs won the ARCA Menards Series in 2021. Joe Gibbs, a former NFL coach and current NASCAR team owner, is his grandfather.

    Is Ty Gibbs related to Joe Gibbs?

    Gibbs is the grandson of Joe Gibbs, the former Washington Redskins head coach and owner of Joe Gibbs Racing, as well as the son of Coy Gibbs, a former NASCAR racer, and Heather Gibbs, a Charlotte-area realtor.

    He is the cousin of former Appalachian State University quarterback Jackson Gibbs and tight end Miller Gibbs, as well as the nephew of late Joe Gibbs Racing co-owner J. D. Gibbs.
