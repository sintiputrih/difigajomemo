    Early Life and Career Beginnings

    Robert Downey Jr. was born on April 4, 1965, in Manhattan, New York, into a family deeply rooted in the entertainment industry. His father, Robert Downey Sr., was an actor and filmmaker, while his mother, Elise Ann, was an actress.

    Downey Jr.'s foray into acting began at a young age, and he quickly gained recognition for his talent.

    Marvel Movies
