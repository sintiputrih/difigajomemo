    The Voice top 8 contestants were revealed on Dec 1, 2021, and Joshua Vacanti made it to the list. One of the two remaining artists from team John Legend, Joshua’s chances at winning the show even looked stronger than ever. 

    But did you know he was married? Yes, his wife Sam Olewnik was cheering from their home as his name was announced. This Joshua Vacanti Bio explores all about their relationship and also sheds light on Joshua’s parents and height. 

    Who Is Joshua Vacanti Wife, Sam Olewnik?

    Joshua Vacanti started dating his wife Sam Olewnik before 2016, and the two went on to get engaged in Jun 2018. A year later, the couple then exchanged vows on November 29, 2019, in an intimate wedding ceremony.

    On their second wedding anniversary, Joshua took it to her IG to write, “Happy Anniversary bubs. I got your back, you got mine.”

    If you haven’t noticed, Joshua often referred to Sam as his “bubs.”

    Also, during his time on The Voice, Joshua made an IG post sharing that he was missing his wife, captioned, “Lucky(+ missing my bubs)”

    As of 2021, the duo looked happiest and was in love more than ever. They shared a passion for singing, and maybe this was also the secret of their happy married life. The last we saw the pair, they were recording the track “I’m you”, written by Doug Sylvester, Phil Jackson, and Bucy boy.

    Also, at the time they were content singing at worship services at the Chapel at Crosspoint and the Chapel at Lockport. They even were in a small worship band that also included Joshua’s mother and stepfather.

    But music wasn’t the only thing that kept the couple connected. Every September, Joshua, and Sam would cosplay different characters, and post them on their IG. From cartoons like Peter Pan to TV shows like The Office, the duo has cosplayed them all.

    The Office was the lovebird’s favorite show, “When you’re a kid, you assume your parents are soulmates. My kids are gonna be right about that,” Joshua once quoted the show over his IG.

    Find Sam over IG @sam_olewnik and Facebook @sam.olewnik.

    Joshua Vacanti On The Voice

    Joshua Vacanti stepped into the show with a bang performing “Into the Unknown” from the movie Frozen. Then for the battle round, he sang “Good 4 U” by Olivia Rodrigo, and went on to sing “Falling” by Harry Styles in the Knockouts.

    Moving to the live playoff, he performed Lady Gaga’s “You and I” and made it to the top 13.

    After a few other amazing performances, Joshua finally then made it to the top 8. He was the last two contestants from team John Legend, and the other was Jershika Maple (who John stole from Kelly).

    Other top 8 finalists were Girl Named Tom, Hailey Mia,  Jim, and Sasha Allen, Lana Scott, Paris Winningham, and Wendy Moten.

    Did you know: Joshua tried a few times to get into America’s Got Talent and American Idol, but never succeeded.

    Joshua Vacanti Job

    Well, Joshua’s main practice is singing. But besides that, he worked as a prevention educator where he taught kids about drug prevention and encouraged them to set goals to accomplish what they want.

    Also, one of the reasons he came to The Voice was to heed the advice he told his students. The guy loved his job dealy and shared that he enjoyed giving others the support he wished he had when he was in school.

    In case you’re curious, a prevention educator’s salary was around $40 thousand per annum in 2021.

    As for his education, Joshua is a Lockport High School class of 2012 graduate. He then transferred to Buffalo State from Niagara County Community College and studied privately with Buffalo State associate professor Holly Bewlay and Victoria Furby.

    “Josh was a wonderful student, committed and dedicated to his craft,” Victoria shared about her former student.

    How Old Is Joshua Vacanti?

    The Voice star, Joshua was 28 years of age when he appeared on The Voice in 2021.

    Joshua Vacanti Parents

    Joshua Vacanti’s biological parents are long separated. He then grew up with his mother Diane Susan Sullivan, and his stepfather Kevin Sullivan. They all were members of the Assembly of God Church in Lockport.

    As for his siblings, Joshua has a sister named Alexandra Vacanti.

    Trivia: It was Joshua’s uncle who decided to register Josh online for The Voice.

    Joshua Vacanti Height

    Joshua stands tall at a height of 5 feet 11 inches (180 cm).

    Mentioning his distinct features, Joshua has a babyface, wears prescription glasses, and has an oblong-shaped face.

    Also, believe it or not, this lean man was once a chubby guy. Back when he was 8, he developed severe and chronic asthma and was prescribed steroids for his condition. Then, he started gaining weight rapidly and became 136 kg (300 lbs) in 2012.
