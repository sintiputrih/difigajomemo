    However, the Duke and Duchess of Sussex have now moved, and Prince William and Kate Middleton have already visited the couple's new home as the royals begin to settle in.

    Stepping out together to attend an Anzac Day service, in honor of Australians and New Zealanders who have died during war and conflict, Harry and Kate put any Kensington Palace split rumors to bed. Plus, they managed to have some exceedingly adorable moments together that you might have missed, proving that their friendship as in-laws is well and truly intact.

    In one particularly sweet moment, Harry could be seen gazing at Kate, and there was so much warmth in his expression, it's clear that they're still close:
