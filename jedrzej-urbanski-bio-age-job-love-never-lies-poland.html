    Netflix’s Love Never Lies Poland: Are Jędrzej Urbański And Damian Bruno Wolny Still Together?

    “Six couples test their trust with an eye-scanning lie detector in this reality series where deception costs money, and the truth comes with a prize,” reads the official description of Netflix’s Love Never Lies Poland.

    Maja Bohosiewicz, an actor and influencer who rose to stardom through her performance shows and films like M Jak Milosc and Pani Z Pzedszkola, is the host of Netflix’s first Polish unscripted reality drama. Lily and Andrew, Kasia and Cornel, Ida and Matthew, Wiktoria and Jasiek, and Bernadette and Dominic are the other 5 couples appearing on this season of Love Never Lies Poland and aiming to score big by remaining honest with each other during their time in the series.

    Jedrzej and Bruno are the exact definition of “opposites attract” as both possess different characters. However, they take their differences in character as the key to a successful relationship, which is why they have been together for the last 3 years.

    Both Jedrzej and Bruno work in the art industry, and it is their passion for art that unites them. The young couple came to Love Never Lies Poland to test the strength of their feelings, answer a few questions, and open up to the next steps in a relationship.

    Ahead of the show’s premiere, Bruno took to his Facebook to share the trailer of Love Never Lies: Poland and write, “✨ Attention! ✨Important information I wanted to share. Together with J liśmydrzej, we took part in the First Polish Reality NETFLIX ♥️ Premiere on 01/25/2023. I invite you to catch a preview.”

    Coming to the question if Jedrzej and Bruno were still together in 2023, we think they’re still a couple. In fact, Jedrzex has also commented “Oooh I It’s in the air ! In the air… How cute!” on Bruno’s Facebook profile picture, which he updated on April 22, 2022.

    Jędrzej Urbański Age

    Reportedly born in 1994, Jedrzej Urbanski’s age was probably 28 at the time of this writing.

    Jędrzej Urbański Job

    Talking about Jedrzej Urbanski’s job, he is a makeup artist by profession, who has been working at Yves Saint Laurent since September 2017. From September 2017 to December 2017, he was a beauty expert at YSL and since January 2018, he has been working as a junior makeup artist.

    Prior to working at Yves Saint Laurent, Jedrzej was a Consultant/Make-up artist at SEPHORA.

    The Love Never Lies: Poland star appeared on the page of VIVA magazine in March 2018. Jedrzej Urbanski shared the news through his professional Facebook handle. Furthermore, Jedrzej was also on SEPHORA TREND REPORT in Arkadia March 29-31, where people could sign up for a 45-minute consultation and makeup with Yves Saint Laurent’s junior makeup artist.

    The reality TV star completed his education at the Higher School of Art – Warsaw School of Arts. He has a Bachelor’s degree in Costume and Stage Production.

    While we don’t know the exact salary of Jedrzej as a make-up artist, the average salary for the same position in Poland is around $1101 monthly. We assume Jedrzej makes somewhere around that figure.

    Jedrzej’s partner, Damian Bruno Wolny, is a hairdresser by profession. Bruno shares pictures of his work on his @damianbruno_hairdesign IG handle.
