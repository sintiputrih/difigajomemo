    Cyrus Roberts Vance (March 27, 1917 – January 12, 2002) was an American lawyer who served as Secretary of State under President Jimmy Carter from 1977 to 1980. He was born in New York City and died in Washington, D.C. Prior to that, he served as the Secretary of the Army and the Deputy Secretary of Defense, among other positions. During his tenure as Secretary of State, Vance handled foreign policy with a focus on negotiation rather than confrontation, with a particular interest in weapons reduction. During Operation Eagle Claw, a covert operation to rescue American hostages held in Iran, Vance resigned in April 1980, citing political reasons. Edmund Muskie took over as his replacement in the post. Vance was the cousin (and adopted son) of John W.
