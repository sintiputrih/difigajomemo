    Melty Blood Type Lumina: Latest News, Trailer, Character Roster & More

    Melty Blood developers Type-Moon had been steadily promoting Melty Blood Type Lumina before its release last September, but thanks to DLC, the fight's not over yet.

    Fans have long been waiting for this release for almost two decades. Here's what we know about Melty Blood Type Lumina's upcoming plans.

    Read More: Guilty Gear Strive Review: A Welcoming Fighting Game?

    Melty Blood: Type Lumina - Latest News

    January 3, 2022

    Type-Moon Games has confirmed that the first set of DLC characters will be free for Melty Blood Type Lumina. Players will be getting Dead Apostle Noel and Aoko Aozaki for free. The new DLC drops on January 13, 2022. After this, the next set of characters is officially confirmed for Spring 2022 release.

    Melty Blood: Type Lumina -Trailers

    As seen on Type-Moon's official Youtube channel, the developers have been releasing a steady stream of new Melty Blood Type Lumina trailers. Since July, Type-Moon has been revealing at least two character trailers per week. More characters may be revealed for this title soon as the game's release date closes in. For now, here's the release announcement trailer to give you a better look at the game.
