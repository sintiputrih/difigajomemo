    An Exceptional Quarterback

    Throughout his tenure at UCF and subsequently at Oklahoma, Dillon Gabriel has carved out a reputation as an extraordinary quarterback. His remarkable precision in passing and agility on the field has garnered widespread acclaim. Gabriel’s impact on both teams has been profound, highlighting his exceptional abilities and cementing his role as a cornerstone player. His journey commenced at Mililani High School in Hawaii, where he attained remarkable triumphs, earning the prestigious title of Gatorade High School Football Player of the Year for Hawaii in 2018. With an illustrious career spanning numerous seasons and surpassing 14,000 passing yards, Gabriel has emerged as one of the most coveted quarterbacks in collegiate football.

    Joining the Oregon Ducks

    In his quest for a new team to elevate his NFL prospects, Dillon Gabriel has revealed his decision to transfer to the Oregon Ducks for his last season in college football. After weighing multiple options, he concluded that Oregon offered the perfect fit. The Ducks are gearing up for a transition from the Pac-12 to the Big Ten in the forthcoming season, and Gabriel’s addition is poised to bolster their quarterback lineup significantly.

    Poised for Success

    Dillon Gabriel’s move from Oklahoma to the Oregon Ducks is fueled by both coaching alterations and his aspiration for NFL success. With his exceptional abilities and a stellar track record, he stands poised to create a substantial impact in his concluding college football season, setting the stage for a prosperous journey into professional football.
