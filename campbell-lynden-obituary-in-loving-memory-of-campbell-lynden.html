    Campbell Lynden Obituary, Death –  Campbell Lynden, departed this world suddenly on 25th December 2023, at her home in Hugomont Park, Ballymena. She was the beloved daughter of the late Agnes (Nan) and Campbell, and a dear sister to John, Karen, and the late Campbell and Rosamund. Campbell’s unexpected passing has left a void in the hearts of those who knew and loved her.

    Her family and friends are grappling with the sudden loss of a cherished sibling and friend. Funeral arrangements are yet to be finalized, and further details will be communicated at a later time. In lieu of family flowers, the Lynden family kindly requests that donations be sent to James Henry Funeral Services.

    100 Broughshane Street, Ballymena BT43 6EE, for the Northern Ireland Chest Heart and Stroke Association. These contributions will serve as a lasting tribute to Campbell’s memory, supporting a cause that was close to her heart. The family has requested privacy during this difficult time, and the house is to remain private as they come to terms with their grief.

    Campbell will be lovingly remembered by her surviving brother, sister, and the entire family circle. Her warm presence, infectious laughter, and the love she shared with those around her will be greatly missed. As the Lynden family navigates through this time of sorrow, they find solace in the memories of a life well-lived. Campbell Lynden will be greatly loved and now greatly missed by all who had the privilege of knowing her. May she rest in peace, surrounded by the eternal love that continues to resonate in the hearts of those she touched.
