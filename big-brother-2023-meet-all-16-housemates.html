    Big Brother 2023: Meet All 16 Housemates – Big Brother 2023 is the highly anticipated twentieth installment of the Big Brother series.

    Big Brother 2023 holds particular significance as it is slated to make its debut on ITV2, with ITV securing the broadcasting rights in August 2022, culminating almost four years since its last airing on Channel 5.

    The hosting duties for this exciting series will be shared by AJ Odudu and Will Best, who will also take the helm of the companion show, Big Brother: Late & Live.

    In terms of production, reports surfaced in April 2022 about discussions between ITV Studios and Banijay, the production company holding the rights to Big Brother, contemplating a revival in 2023 after a hiatus of five years.

    The official confirmation came on August 1, 2022, during the final of the eighth series of Love Island. A 20-second teaser trailer aired on ITV2, officially announcing the show’s return for a new series in 2023. ITV becomes the third network to acquire the rights to the series, following its cancellations on Channel 4 in 2010 and Channel 5 in 2018.

    Big Brother 2023: Meet All 16 Housemates

    Big Brother 2023 is currently underway, and it commenced on October 8, 2023. The show has a total of 16 housemates who will be competing for the grand prize. Below are the names and other details of the Big Brother 2023 housemates.

    Chanelle

    Chanelle is a 29-year-old lady and she is a Dental therapist. She hails from Llanelli.

    Dylan

    Dylan is 39 years old and he is a native of Coventry. He is a renowned disc jockey (DJ).

    Farida Khalifa

    Farida Khalifa is known to be the oldest among all the housemates. She is 50 years old and she hails from Wolverhampton. She is known to be a Make-up artist.

    Hallie

    Hallie is the youngest of all the housemates, and she is 18 years old. She is based in London and she is a Youth Worker.

    Henry Southan

    Henry Southan is 25 years old and he is from Cotswolds. He is known to be a food writer.

    Jenkin

    Jenkin is known to be a vibrant 25-year-old young guy from Bridgend. He is a barman and cleaner.

    Jordan

    For Jordan, he is 25 years old, hails from Scunthorpe, and is a lawyer.

    Noky Simbani is an indigene of Derby, and she is 26 years old. She is a banker by profession and was once a contestant for Miss Universe GB.
