    George and Amal Clooney looked like Old Hollywood perfection at the U.K. premiere of The Tender Bar.

    The pair made a rare red-carpet appearance together at the 2021 BFI London Film Festival where Amal simply stunned in a white sequin-embellished strapless gown, a coordinating feather boa designed by 16Arlington, and diamond earrings by high jeweler Moussaieff. The human rights attorney wore her hair in her signature flowing waves, with bold brows and a red lip.

    Karwai Tang//Getty Images

    George beamed at his wife and held her close as they made their way down the red carpet, and the actor even helped out when she had a small fashion snafu take place. Amal's dress seemed to have snagged against her stiletto, leaving George to hold onto his wife as nearby handlers helped unhook the hem of her dress from her shoe.

    NIKLAS HALLE&apos;N//Getty Images

    According to People, the pair have spent recent months at their Lake Como home with their four-year-old twins, daughter Ella and son Alexander.

    "George and Amal took every day as it came. They have always prioritized family time, so they were very positive about all the extra time with the kids," a source told the outlet. "They are the most amazing parents. George is such a fun dad. The kids adore him."

    The couple have also reportedly taught their children Italian in recent years, according to George.

    "They speak fluent Italian, but Amal and I do not," George told reporters at the premiere. "That's a flaw in our logic, right there."
