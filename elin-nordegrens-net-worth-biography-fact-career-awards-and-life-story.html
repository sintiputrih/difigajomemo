    Elin Nordegren Wiki – Biography

    Elin Nordegren is the Swedish-born ex-wife of professional golfer Tiger Woods. She has worked as a model and nanny. Nordegren worked as a cashier in supermarkets to finance her studies. In 2000, Nordegren started modeling and appeared on the cover of Cafe Sport magazine in the summer of 2000. Nordegren graduated from Rollins College in Winter Park, Florida, with a degree in psychology, in 2014 receiving the outstanding senior award.

    Elin Nordegren Age

    She was born on 1 January 1980 in Stockholm, Sweden.

    Family

    She is the daughter of Thomas Nordegren and Barbro Holmberg. Her mother, Barbro Holmberg, is a politician and the former Swedish migration and asylum policy minister, and the former Governor of Gävleborg County. Her father, Thomas Nordegren, is a radio journalist who served as bureau chief in Washington, D.C. Nordegren has an elder brother, Axel, and a twin sister, Josefin. Nordegren has three kids Sam Alexis Woods Charlie Axel Woods and Arthur Nordegren Cameron.

    Elin Nordegren Marriage

    Nordegren and Tiger Woods were married in 2004 and had two kids, Sam Alexis Woods, and Charlie Axel Woods. In 2009, her marriage to Woods was the subject of extensive media coverage after Woods admitted to infidelity. In 2010, Nordegren and Tiger Woods finalized their divorce in court in Panama City. Nordegren received $100 million from Tiger Woods after the divorce as a settlement fee for the divorce. After the divorce, Nordegren also had a relationship with the late coal magnate and philanthropist Chris Cline. Nordegren gave birth to a son in October 2019 with the former NFL player Jordan Cameron, Arthur Nordegren Cameron.

    Height

    She stands at a height of 5 feet and 10 inches (1.8m) tall.

    Net Worth

    She has an estimated net worth of $200 million.

    Nationality

    She is of Swedish nationality.
