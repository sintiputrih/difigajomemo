    Josh Duhamel is engaged!

    The 49-year-old Life As We Know It actor proposed to girlfriend Audra Mari on her 28th birthday on Saturday (January 8) after more than two years of dating.

    Click inside to read more…

    “It’s on!! She found a message in a bottle that washed ashore and said YES!!” Josh wrote on Instagram.

    Along with his announcement, Josh shared a photo of he and the former Miss World America winner shortly after he proposed to her at sunset at the beach.

    Josh and Audra were first spotted out to together in October 2019 after several months of dating on the low.

    If you didn’t know, Josh was previously married to Fergie from 2009 until 2019. They share 8-year-old son Axl. Audra previously dated Property Brothers star Jonathan Scott from 2013 until 2015.

    Congrats Josh and Audra!
