    Top 10 celebrity fitness DVDs

      Kym Marsh: Power Sculpt

      The Hear'Say singer releases her first fitness DVD on 26 December, which promises to take you from "flab to firm" using six workouts. Kim also combines cardiovascular and strength training so that the body continues to burn fat for up to 36 hours after the workout.

      Davina McCall: 7 Minute Fit Part 2

      With 12 bestselling workout videos to her name, we've singled out her most recent DVD, which was released in July 2015. The seven-minute workouts are designed for those with hectic lifestyles, helping them burn fat and get fit in just 420 seconds.

      Darcey Bussell: Pilates For Life

      Despite being released nine years ago, Darcey's pilates workout still has one of the highest ratings online and consistently sells copies year after year. The retired ballerina released a book to accompany the DVD which also received rave reviews.

      Jillian Anderson: Beginner Shred

      trainer is known for her no-nonsense approach to fitness, which shines througout her workout DVDs. We've recommended as it builds up your fitness levels with three 20-minute workouts that get harder every week.

      Mel B: Totally Fit

      There's something for everyone in the former Spice Girls' DVD. There's an hours worth of cardio and resistance training on offer, or ten minute slots of interval training for when you're pressed for time.

      Tracy Anderson: Slim Plus Sculpt Cardio with Band

      With a number of celebrity clients including Madonna, Gwyneth Paltrow and Shakira, it's clear Tracy knows what she's doing. The personal trainer has several videos to choose from, but the use of a resistance band in the DVD helps to break up the workout.

      Lorraine Kelly: Walk Off The Pounds

      Although there are some who do not consider walking to be a form of strenuous exercise, power-walking can burn the same amount of calories as jogging or running. Lorraine helps with demonstrating some power walking routines that will help you form your stride.

      Kirsty Gallacher: Body Sculpt

      The star's DVD helps with toning and sculpting the muscles using a variety of different exercises. Kirsty has always had a passion for fitness and is currently training to become a personal trainer, so look out for any new videos she may release!

      Jane Fonda: Original Workout

      The original fitness guru from the 1980s rereleased her iconic workout tape in DVD format, and whilst the routines may be more than 30 years old, they're still just as effective. At the time of release, Jane's video became a top seller for several years, and looking back at it now it's easy to see why.

      Jennifer Ellison: Fat Blaster Workout

      The model released her popular workout DVD after the birth of her first child, which saw her put on five stone. She targets problem areas with four separate 'blasting' techniques which focus on using weights, boxing, squats and ab exercises. Jennifer had her third child last year and is now back in the gym, putting her routines to use again.
