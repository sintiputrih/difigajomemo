    Megan Fox is continuing her sultry fashion streak, and she doesn’t care who’s mad about it.

    The Jennifer’s Body alum recently shared new photos of herself posing in another heart-racing outfit. This time, the ensemble consisted of a sheer mesh Jean Paul Gaultier dress with ombré orange, pink, and green stripes all over. She accessorized the risqué look with stacks of silver and bedazzled choker necklaces, plus strappy beige sandals and a bright white manicure.

    Considering how see-through the dress is, Fox appropriately opted to wear nude cover-ups underneath, including a pair of sculpted high-rise underwear and nipple pasties. She acknowledged the look in her caption, writing, “Before you start angrily typing- those are not my nipples, calm down.”

    She also joked, “They are safely and completely hidden under nipple covers so no worries, we will all live another day without a rip in the space-time continuum.”

    Over the past few weeks, Fox has been sharing multiple head-turning outfits on Instagram.

    Most recently, the actor posted snaps from a sunset beach photo shoot, during which she posed in another completely sheer look. This time, it featured a mesh long-sleeved bodycon dress in black with a huge cutout across her chest and torso, revealing a metallic silver bikini underneath.

    Last week, Fox also shared pictures from a different shoot, by a creek, where she embraced cottagecore in a white corset maxi dress with frilly lace trim and lace-up details on the bodice.
