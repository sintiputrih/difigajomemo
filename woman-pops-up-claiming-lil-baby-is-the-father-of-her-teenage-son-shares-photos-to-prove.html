    An American woman identified as Shi Anderson has popped up online claiming multiple award-winning rapper, Lil Baby is the father of her son.

    The lady took to the visual platform, Instagram to unleash a series of posts as she posted photos of Lil Baby when he was a young boy alongside the photos of her son showing the resemblance between the rapper and her son.

    Shi Anderson however tagged Lil Baby telling him to follow the necessary steps to claim his son and these posts have sparked a new discussion on the gram.

    Lil Baby is worth $4 million (N1.6bn) and he’s currently having two children from different women.

    The woman sharing the photos of the rapper and her son captioned, “I can’t keep this a secret any Longer @Libaby needs to come take care of his responsibilities! I am not bitter, my son deserves to know who his father is. #Firstborr”

    She however posted another photo of her teenage son holding wads of cash claiming she’s not hunting for money because they already have enough
