    What happened to Mike Ross in Suits? His Departure Explained

    Its never easy to say goodbye to a cherished character, particularly if they depart a TV show before its finale. Fans of the popular legal drama series Suits on the USA Network felt precisely that when Patrick J. Adamss character, Mike Ross, left the show following the end of Season 7.

    16 Mar 2024, 00:00

    It’s never easy to say goodbye to a cherished character, particularly if they depart a TV show before its finale.

    Fans of the popular legal drama series “Suits” on the USA Network felt precisely that when Patrick J. Adams’s character, Mike Ross, left the show following the end of Season 7.

    We’ll look at Mike Ross’s reasons for leaving “Suits” and the way the plot handled his exit in this article.

    Mike Ross: The Character and the Journey

    Mike Ross was first featured in the “Suits” pilot episode, and he quickly established himself as a key character.

    A troubled young adult with an amazing photographic memory, Mike was kicked out of college but managed to support his grandmother’s medical expenses by taking LSAT exams for other people.

    His adventure started when he happened to run across Harvey Spectre, who employed him to work at the New York City law firm Pearson Hardman.

    What happened to Mike Ross in Suits?

    The departure of Mike Ross, aka Patrick J. Adams, was revealed at the conclusion of Season 7 of “Suits.” When Mike married Rachel Zane, who was portrayed by Meghan Markle, and they decided to move from New York to Seattle, a major turning point in Mike’s life occurred.

    Their characters’ journey came to an end when they established their own legal firm there, giving them a feeling of closure and a “happily ever after”.

    Fans felt mixed emotions when they parted ways with the series. Due to her upcoming responsibilities as a member of the British Royal Family, Meghan Markle departed “Suits.” Regarding Patrick J. Adams, he thought the tale of Mike Ross was over.

    In an interview with The Hollywood Reporter, Adams discussed his thoughts about quitting the program and listed a number of factors that influenced his choice.

    Personal Reasons: Adams clarified that he felt compelled to spend time with his family after spending seven seasons away from home and his wife. His decision to leave the show was driven by his wish to spend more time with his loved ones, as the responsibilities of a long-running TV show had become too much for him personally.

    Story Arc: Adams expressed doubts regarding the future directions Mike Ross’s character could take from a storytelling standpoint. He brought up the fact that Mike had promised during his trial that, he made be pledge to practise law in the future, he would serve the interests of impoverished people rather than those of corporations. Adams wondered how this character journey would work into the overall story of the show.

    End of an Era: Adams thought that leaving the program was the right option, even though it wasn’t an easy one to make. The actor conveyed a desire for resolution and to investigate novel prospects beyond “Suits,” encompassing both personal and professional domains.

    Mike Ross’s Comeback in Season 9

    When Mike Ross left the show in Season 7, it appeared as though his career was over, but in the ninth and final season of “Suits,” Patrick J. Adams surprised everyone by coming back.

    Fans were thrilled when a favorite character was decided to return for the last season of the show.

    Mike came back, however, it was only to play a periodic part. He was brought back to New York City by one of his cases, which was the central plot point of his comeback.

    The final season gained complexity and a compelling dynamic as a result of him finding himself at war with his former law practice due to this case.

    Adams told Entertainment Tonight that he decided to rejoin the cast and crew and celebrate the show’s legacy when he was asked why he decided to return to the series.

    It was an opportunity to “get the band back together” for a final tour, as he put it. For Adams, it was a chance to witness the last moments of the program and bid his character and the series the proper farewell.

    A Farewell Sendoff: Mike Ross Comes Back for Final Words

    “Suits” was able to provide a fitting finale for both Mike Ross and the series as a whole thanks to Patrick J. Adams’s comeback for the last season.

    The complexities of Mike’s return to the company and his interactions with his former coworkers, especially his mentor Harvey Spectre, might be covered in the series with his presence.

    In addition to giving fans one last opportunity to see Mike Ross, the character’s comeback was noteworthy since it furthered the main plot.

    The return of Mike Ross felt like a logical continuation of the story’s resolution since Harvey and Donna had made the decision to go to Seattle to collaborate with Mike and Rachel in their new legal practice.

    In conclusion, Mike Ross’s unforgettable adventure

    Mike Ross had an incredible journey on “Suits,” replete with growth on a personal level, obstacles in the legal system, and lifelong friendships.

    For viewers who had been emotionally invested in the character, his exit from the program during Season 7 was bittersweet.

    But when Patrick J. Adams reappeared in a recurring role in the last season, Mike’s tale came to a suitable end, and the program was able to commemorate its remarkable nine-season career.

    He is known for his intelligence, honesty, and the lasting impression he made on the television series “Suits.”
