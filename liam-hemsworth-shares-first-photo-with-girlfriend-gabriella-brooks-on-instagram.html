    09 Sep 2024, 00:00

    Liam Hemsworth and Gabriella Brooks are making their first public appearance as a couple!

    Earlier this week, the 31-year-old Hunger Games actor and the 25-year-old Australian model attended the 2021 Golden Dinner, which supports the Sydney Children’s Hospital, in Australia.

    A few days after the event, Liam took to Instagram to share a few photos from the charity event, marking the first time he has ever shared photos with Gabriella on his social media.

    Click inside to see the post!

    In one photo, Liam and Gabriella have their arms wrapped around each other as they pose with his older brother Chris Hemsworth and wife Elsa Pataky and a few friends.

    Liam also shared a selfie from their table, featuring Gabriella, Chris, and Elsa.

    “Fantastic night raising much needed funds and awareness for one of the most important and challenging issues, children’s mental health. Thank you @itsmondotcom for hosting the evening and all you do for the Sydney children’s hospital @sydney_kids #golddinner,” Liam captioned the post.

    If you didn’t know, Liam and Gabriella have been very quietly dating since late 2019.

    The two have been hanging out in their native Australia during the on-going pandemic.
