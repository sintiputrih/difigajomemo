    30 Aug 2024, 00:00

    Budding Nigerian recording artiste and sensational performer, Bella Alubo join forces with Keziah Mallam to dish out a fine tune labeled; “Green Eyed Monster” produced by Tuan Malik.

    The record was lifted off her debut studio album, ‘Bella Buffet’. The body of work comes in a package of 17 solid track with vocal assistance from some of your favorite Nigerian musicians.

    On this new album, Bella Alubo infuses her versatility over a myriad of melodies and relatable stories, condensed through the singers dynamic sweet, husky and silky vocals.

    Bella Alubo is well known for her song; “Agbani Remix” featuring Zlatan Ibile. She was nominated for the promising act to watch at the 2018 Nigeria Entertainment Awards and was also listed among 2018 promising artists.

    Bella Alubo was formerly signed under Tinny Entertainment, she left the label in September 2018. She describes her music as a cocktail of her influences which include afrobeat, hip-hop, smooth jazz, R&B and reggae.

    ALSO: Bella Alubo – Little Bum Bum
