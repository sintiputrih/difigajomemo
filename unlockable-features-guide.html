    Punishing Gray Raven is a visually-stunning, action-packed ARPG set in a post-apocalyptic world overrun by the Corrupted. The game has various milestones and content that need to be unlocked by progressing through the game, usually by clearing story missions and leveling up your Commandant. In our Punishing Gray Raven Story Milestones and Unlockables guide, we will list down all unlockable features in the game.

    Your Commandant Level is your player level. As your constructs clear missions, you’ll also earn a corresponding amount of EXP. Your commandant level and story progress will determine what features you can unlock and take part of. Features that are locked behind game progression actually makes sense since the game doesn’t want you to go tackling high-level content while you’re inexperienced and not without a reliable team to go with.

    Upon starting the game, it is highly recommended to play regularly and do all activities you can to reach LV35 and clear story level 5-7 Normal as soon as you can. This will allow you to unlock all Weekly Missions which can give you a reliable source of Black Cards (the game’s premium currency).
