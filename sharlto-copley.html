    Sharlto Copley Biography

    Sharlto Copley is best known as Actor, producer, director who has an estimated Net Worth of $15 Million. Gained recognition as the star of the Oscar-nominated film District 9. He also portrayed Agent M. Kruger in Elysium and Stefan in the 2014 film Maleficent.  He was born on November 27,1973, Pretoria, South Africa. He started out as a television producer in South Africa and appeared on The A-Team. He became one of South Africa's youngest television producers. He co-starred as H.M. Murdock with Patrick Wilson, who played Lynch on the film The A-Team. His brother gained fame as a member of the band Hotwater. He married Tanit Phoenix in 2016. His zodiac sign is Sagittarius.
