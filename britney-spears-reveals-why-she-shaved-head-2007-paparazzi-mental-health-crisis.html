    Britney Spears Finally Addresses Why She Shaved Her Head In 2007 As Her Mental Health Is Questioned

    Britney Spears mental health is currently being questioned in response to her bizarre online behavior, but the singer recently opened up about a previous mental health crisis she went through nearly two decades ago when she infamously shaved her head in front of paparazzi.

    21 Aug 2024, 00:00

    Highlights

    • Britney Spears shaved her head in 2007 as an act of rebellion due to feeling scrutinized about her appearance throughout her life.
    • She reveals in her memoir that she terminated a pregnancy with Justin Timberlake after feeling pressured by him, despite wanting a family with him in the future.
    • Justin Timberlake is reportedly nervous about what Britney will reveal about their relationship, as she plans to release a sequel to her memoir.

    Britney Spears’ mental health is currently being questioned in response to her bizarre online behavior, but the singer recently opened up about a previous mental health crisis she went through nearly two decades ago when she infamously shaved her head in front of paparazzi.

    The singer addressed the 2007 incident in her new memoir The Woman In Me, releasing later this month.

    Britney’s Head-Shaving Incident Was An Act Of Rebellion, She Claims

    “I’d been eyeballed so much growing up. I’d been looked up and down, had people telling me what they thought of my body, since I was a teenager. Shaving my head and acting out were my ways of pushing back,” a sample of the book reads, PEOPLE reports.

    Britney was placed in a conservatorship the following year, admitting she lost control over most of her personal freedom as a consequence, including how her hair looked. She also lost primary custody of her two sons.

    “I was made to understand that those days were now over,” she wrote. “I had to grow my hair out and get back into shape. I had to go to bed early and take whatever medication they told me to take.”

    During the 2007 incident, Britney walked into a California hair salon and requested her then-brown hair to be buzzed off. When the salon owner apparently refused to comply, Britney proceeded to grab a pair of clippers and do the deed herself.

    Onlooking paparazzi got a series of footage of the singer shaving her head, which came only days before Britney attacked a photographer’s car with an umbrella.

    Britney Said She Terminated A Pregnancy With Justin Timberlake

    Britney makes a series of startling revelations in her memoir, including about her family and past relationship. Another striking confession is that she became pregnant during her relationship with Justin Timberlake, but ultimately decided to end the pregnancy after receiving pressure from him.

    “I loved Justin so much. I always expected us to have a family together one day. This would just be much earlier than I’d anticipated,” the same excerpt from PEOPLE reads.

    “But Justin definitely wasn’t happy about the pregnancy. He said we weren’t ready to have a baby in our lives, that we were way too young,” the former Disney star went on.

    Britney didn’t confirm how old she was when the abortion took place, but it’s speculated she would’ve been in her late teens.

    Justin hasn’t formally responded to Britney’s claims, but a report from earlier in the week claimed the singer was nervous about what his ex would reveal. “He’s very curious what she’ll reveal from their relationship,” a source told Page Six. “It’s eating at him.”
