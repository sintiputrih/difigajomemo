    Naomi walked out on WWE along with Mercedes Mone a year ago on Monday Night RAW and fans wondered if she would ever come back. Of course, that’s not happening as she is now part of Impact Wrestling. Now going as Trinity, she has been more than happy in the company. Now it appears Trinitu flaunted her fashion skills with an outfit inspired by Beyonce.

    Trinity has been at the very top of her game after defeating Deonna Purrazzo at Impact Wrestling’s Slammiversary event to become the new Knockouts Champion. Trinity has been able to express herself better following her win, especially when it comes to her fashion choices.

    The Impact Wrestling star took to Twitter and uploaded a couple of photos of herself wearing an outfit inspired by Beyonce’s legendary Rennaisance tour. Naturally, Trinity looked right at home with the look.

    Trinity also achieved unique feat after Impact Wrestling’s 1000th episode. Trinity will continue to spread her wings and show the world who she is and that’s all that matters to her now.
