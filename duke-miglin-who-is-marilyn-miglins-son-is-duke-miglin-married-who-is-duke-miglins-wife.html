    Duke Miglin is the son Marilyn Miglin and Lee Miglin. Both his parents are dead. His father, Lee Miglin was an American business tycoon and philanthropist. After starting his career as a door-to-door salesman and then broker, Miglin became a successful real estate developer. He was murdered in his home in May 1997 by Andrew Cunanan, a spree killer.

    Who is Marilyn Miglin’s son?

    Duke Miglin was born in 1971 and later became an actor. He has three film credits: Air Force One (1997), Cyberdorm (1999), and Mulligans! (1987), and none since. In May 2017, ABC reported that Duke had followed in his father’s footsteps and become a real estate developer.

    Is Duke Miglin married?

    Duke Miglin might be married but he has remained silent on it in the public. All the interviews he has granted have been centered on his father’s murder in 1997 and have said nothing about his personal and marital affairs. He is known in the public because of his parents and also featured in some movies.

    Who is Duke Miglin’s wife?

    There is no information on Duke Miglin’s wife. It is not even known whether he is married and has kids.
