      Eddie Redmayne's early life

      Eddie was born on 6 January, 1982, the son of a London banker. At Eton he was in the same year as Prince William, whom he describes as "a wonderful man". From there, he went on to read History of Art at Cambridge University and graduated with 2:1 Honours in 2003.

      Eddie is married to Hannah Bagshawe, whom he met whilst attending Eton College. The couple tied the knot at Babington House in Somerset on 15 December 2014.
