    Lisa Vanderpump is a television personality and businesswoman best known for her appearances on The Real Housewives of Beverly Hills, Vanderpump Rules and Vanderpump Dogs. In addition to her work in the entertainment and restaurant industries, Lisa is also a published author, designer, and works with different kinds of charities. With all her business ventures combined, Lisa has successfully accumulated a total net worth of $90 million throughout the course of her life and career.

    Lisa was born in Dulwich, England, and lived with her family around the world from London, to Monte Carlo, and even the south of France. She began to accrue notable wealth throughout a variety of movie, television, and stage performances, which she then invested in the restaurant and entertainment industries. From her early work in children's films and TV shows, to her current business ventures, here's how Lisa accumulated her $90 million net worth.

    9 How Did Lisa Vanderpump Get Her Money?

    Lisa started making money when she was only nine years old by enrolling full-time in drama school and working in children’s films and TV shows. She made her film debut at the age of thirteen as a character's daughter in the 1973 romantic comedy film titled, A Touch of Class. The film later won an Oscar Award, though Vanderpump herself had nothing to do with the award given to the film. She later appeared in small roles in episodic television programs throughout the 1970s, 1980s, and 1990s, including Silk Stalkings and Baywatch Nights. Vanderpump is a very smart woman, and she used this early success to launch the rest of her career in business and the entertainment industry.

    8 Vanderpump In Dancing With The Stars And Music Videos

    Lisa has been featured in the music videos "Poison Arrow" and "Mantrap" by ABC, "(What) In The Name of Love" by Naked Eyes, and "G.U.Y." by Lady Gaga. She has been in a total of over 100 commercials for brands including Maltesers, Lilt, Britvic 55 and Hamlet cigars. She also appeared on the 16th season of Dancing with the Stars, partnered with professional dancer Gleb Savchenko, and finished the competition in tenth place.

    7 Lisa Vanderpump In The Real Housewives Of Beverly Hills

    In October 2010, Lisa joined Bravo's The Real Housewives of Beverly Hills as one of the original cast members for nine seasons from 2010 to June 2019. According to CheatSheet, Lisa accrued a reported $1 million per season, making her one of the highest-paid stars in the entire Real Housewives franchise. With that salary in addition to her other reality TV shows and spin-offs, Lisa has become one of the highest-paid stars on Bravo overall. Not only did she make a lot of money from the reality show, but The Real Housewives of Beverly Hills also opened the door for her to star in her own television series, Vanderpump Rules.

    6 Vanderpump Rules And Vanderpump Dogs

    Vanderpump Rules is a reality TV series, and spin-off of The Real Housewives of Beverly Hills, about the staff at Lisa’s restaurants and bars. Since premiering on Bravo in 2013, the show has resulted in two spin-offs, Vanderpump Rules After Show and Vanderpump Rules: Jax And Brittany Take Kentucky. Vanderpump Dogs is another spin-off of RHOBH, featuring Lisa as she runs her dog rescue organization, The Vanderpump Dog Foundation, with staff and people adopting pets. Vanderpump Dogs only ran for one season in 2021, but Vanderpump Rules is still going strong.

    5 The Vanderpump Dog Foundation

    The Vanderpump Dog Foundation creates a better world for dogs worldwide by providing essential care and resources in partnership with rescue sanctuaries in different countries. Lisa owns and operates two rescue facilities in China with over 500 dogs rescued from the Yulin Dog Meat Festival and the dog meat trade. In addition to establishing rescue sanctuaries, Lisa is also involved in ventures that feed, vaccinate, and spay and neuter dogs in Peru and Venezuela.

    4 What Restaurants Does Lisa Vanderpump Own?

    Lisa and her husband, Ken Todd, have been in the restaurant business for roughly three decades and continue to command millions in profits each year. Together they have successfully owned and operated over 30 restaurants, bars and clubs across the United Kingdom and the United States. Some of the most famous include The Shadow Lounge, Bar Soho, SUR Restaurant & Lounge, Villa Blanca, Pump Restaurant, Tom Tom Restaurant & Bar, Vanderpump Cocktail Garden and Vanderpump à Paris.

    3 Lisa Vanderpump Invests In Real Estate

    Lisa and Ken, who originally made his fortune in textiles, began acquiring properties as their main source of income and developed a successful business. Lisa said, "My husband and I have designed and built homes and venues across the world …[it] has given us the capacity to create whatever the heart desires. It’s been a dream to see these pieces come to fruition, many of which have been created for our own personal ventures."

    2 The Vanderpump Estate Jewelry Collection

    The Vanderpump Estate Jewelry Collection was first launched in 2015 and debuted on Valentine’s Day weekend in 2022 during EVINE Live's broadcast at The Plaza Hotel in New York City. Lisa told Bravo, "In developing this collection, I wanted to create something that all women could enjoy -- a collection that was both attainable and glamorous." The jewelry collection embodies bold, glamorous looks inspired by her travels around the world and is available at EVINE.

    1 Vanderpump Wine and Liquor

    Lisa created Vanderpump Rosé as a classic Côtes de Provence wine, with all the history and flavors that are offered in the South of France. Lisa also launched a Sonoma County Cabernet paired with an estate-grown Sonoma Coast Chardonnay that are luxurious, elegant, and delicious but still an affordable possibility. Lisa's motto in life is to "find something that you love to do and then you won’t have to work a day in your life." Lisa even hosts events now where she signs her wine bottles.
