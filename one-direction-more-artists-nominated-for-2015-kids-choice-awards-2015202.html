    The fans will decide! Us Weekly can exclusively reveal the musical nominees up for the win at Nickelodeon’s 28th Annual Kids’ Choice Awards, set to go down live on Saturday, March 28, at The Forum in Los Angeles. Hosted by Nick Jonas, stars like Ariana Grande and Katy Perry have the chance to take home an orange blimp while British boy banders One Direction are also in the running.

    “We're over the moon to be nominated for Favorite Music Group at this year's Kids’ Choice Awards!,” One Directions’ Niall Horan tells Us in an exclusive statement. “Thank you to Nickelodeon and of course to our incredible fans.”

    Beginning Friday, Feb. 20, you can cast your vote across 21 categories on nick.com/kca or by tweeting #KCA. Check out the rest of your favorite artists nominated in the three categories below:
