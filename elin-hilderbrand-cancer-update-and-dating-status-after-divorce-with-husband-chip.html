    Elin is a 1991 graduate of Johns Hopkins University. Some of her books are The Sixth Wedding: A 28 Summers Story (2021), Golden Girl (2021), The Surfing Lesson: An Original Short Story (2013), Troubles in Paradise (2020), and The Beach Club (2000).

    What Cancer Does Elin Hilderbrand Have?

    Though Elin Hilderbrand had breast cancer, she is now cancer-free.

    In June 2014, doctors diagnosed her with it. After that, she received a double mastectomy surgery at Massachusetts General Hospital.

    Her "normalcy of life, making pancakes for her family, taking her children to school, and writing novels, kept her going.

    Elin wasn't silent about her treatment. She announced her diagnosis via the Huffington Post. After waking up from the surgery, she felt enormous pain and had reconstruction.

    But in October, she had a life-threatening infection on her left breast implant. Due to it, she had to go to Boston from Nantucket via an emergency helicopter.
