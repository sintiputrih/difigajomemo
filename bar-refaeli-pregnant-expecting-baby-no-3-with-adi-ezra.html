    Round three! Bar Refaeli announced that she and her husband, Adi Ezra, are expecting their third child.

    In the model’s Thursday, June 20, Instagram video captioned, “OOPS I did it again,” TV host Assi Azar revealed that he is going to model for eyewear brand Carolina Lemke. But when he asked the 34-year-old to speak on their partnership, she simply said in Hebrew, “I’m pregnant.”

    After asking the Sports Illustrated Swimsuit model whether she was telling the truth, the Beauty and the Baker creator, 40, smiled and hugged her. “Wasn’t she just pregnant a second ago?” he went on to say, according to the Jerusalem Post. “I’m so happy for her — if it’s true. She looks a little pregnant, she does. Why does she need three kids anyway?”

    Refaeli and her husband, 44, who tied the knot in 2015, already share daughters Liv, 2, and Elle, 20 months.
